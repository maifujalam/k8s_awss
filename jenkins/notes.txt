1.  Set Up a New Webhook
    a. In the repository's Settings, click on Webhooks from the left sidebar.
    b. Click the Add webhook button.
    c. In the Payload URL field, enter the URL of your Jenkins server followed by /github-webhook/. For example: https://jenkins.maifuj.com/github-webhook/
    d. You can click on enable ssl verification (as its ssl verified by lets encrypt),which event to trigger webhook is just push event.
